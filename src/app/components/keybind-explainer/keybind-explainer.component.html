Test mode keybinds:

  • Toggle keybinds list - Backquote
  • Insert KKM - space (kkm screen)
  • Toggle online ticket available - "O" key (kkm screen)
  • Input cash - "1,2,5" for coins, "z,x,c,v,b" for banknote (cash payment screen)
  • Accept/Decline credit card - "A" or "D" (card payment screen only)
